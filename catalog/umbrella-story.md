# BCA Umbrella Story

**Canonical owner:** `BCA-OS`
**Public website:** `Blue-Collar-Apps-Founders`
**Retired showcase surface:** `Blue-Collar-Appz`

This document is the source-of-truth narrative for the Blue Collar Apps umbrella.
Public websites may render or rewrite it for presentation, but the facts should
come back here.

## Root

**BC SOS / BCA OS** is the founder-controlled operating system for Blue Collar
Apps Co. It is the kernel that keeps the portfolio coherent: identity, routing,
policy, memory, credits, apps, agents, and founder-private systems.

## Brain

**Conrad / EXPO** is the company brain and front voice. Conrad has two jobs:

- As expediter, Conrad routes information to the correct app, agent, project,
  database, backlog, or founder-private vault.
- As host, Conrad greets visitors, answers questions, guides tours, and explains
  the ecosystem in plain language.

Claude, Gemini, GLM, local models, and other LLMs are tools Conrad can use. They
are not the parent brain.

## Operating Capabilities

- **Founder intake:** notes, ideas, bugs, staff tasks, decisions, research, and
  secrets enter through Conrad and receive a routing receipt.
- **Policy gates:** launch gates are blockers, not reminders. COPPA, A2P 10DLC,
  TCPA, insurance, and other gates stay explicit.
- **Hybrid memory:** working memory, VaultSpace, NotebookLM Galaxies, and
  founder-private vault stay separated by purpose and sensitivity.
- **Local LLM:** routine intelligence should move toward local/on-site inference
  where privacy, cost, and latency justify it.
- **Credit ledger:** one balance should spend across the ecosystem instead of
  stacking subscriptions per app.
- **App and agent fleet:** every product and standalone agent should plug into
  the same OS, memory, routing, and credit logic.

## Portfolio Lanes

1. **Professional Suite**: operator software for restaurants, venues, food
   trucks, hotels, resorts, culinary pros, margin control, and compliance.
2. **Life Management**: Maestro and VaultSpace as personal command center plus
   secure memory.
3. **Consumer / Dusk Hub**: nightlife, tastings, social coordination, food
   discovery, pubs, valet, and community-facing surfaces.
4. **Platform & Agents**: EXPO OS, Conrad, BrainForge, and the standalone agent
   fleet.
